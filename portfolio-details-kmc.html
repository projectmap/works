Short description

Intraoral X-rays are the most common type of dental X-ray taken. These X-rays provide a lot of detail and allow dentist to find cavities, check the health of the tooth root and bone surrounding the tooth, check the status of developing teeth, and monitor the general health of your teeth and jawbone.
